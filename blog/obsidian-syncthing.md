As someone who went all-in on Obsidian about a year ago, I'm not ecstatic about being back, but the truth is that it has the best mobile interface of any notetaking app that I've tried so far. And pairing this up with Syncthing is just, *muah*. I don't think I'll find myself paying for sync services ever again, local sync is just too flawless. 

Other than that, I've found myself busy again, but with the nice weather it all somehow seems managable. I like it in the evening when puffy clouds sit on the horizon, sticking out in the distance like faraway mountains, such that the pink setting sun colors their rolling faces. I'm not sure why they do that, it's almost like they're slipping off the edge of the world, following the sun.

I've been given an old server by a friend who no longer needs it (if you're reading this, thanks, friend), and eventually I hope to migrate this website to it. That will have to wait until I get an ethernet cord long enough to reach the storage room, as the thing is too loud to keep out in the basement by the router. The only problem with my current setup is how annoying it is to throw a new post up, but it's not too bad.

Well, that's all.
