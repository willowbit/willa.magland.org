About Me

Hi! My name is Willa, my pronouns are she/her, and I'm a history and English student at the University of Delaware. I am a researcher, writer, bookworm, musician, and lifelong Beatles fan. Computer science is also a huge hobby of mine. (So if you're having tech problems, I can probably help you out.)

Around the UD campus, you might find me

  • at the CTF meetings
  • at the Classic Lit Club
  • wandering the Morris stacks
  • ...or hanging around the history undergrad lounge.

This website is a longtime passion project, but also a protest against the dangers of centralized social media like Instagram, Twitter, Reddit, etc. All content on this website is carefully created, published, and hosted with my own two hands. Stay indie!

A note on AI: I do not use generative AI in any of my work, whether personal, academic, or professional. All work that is correctly attributed to me is AI-free to the extent of my knowledge. If you have a question about my work, please contact me.

Site Specs

I started this website in late 2023. It is built with 11ty, served with nginx and apache, and protected by Cloudflare. The previous version of this site was built with Hugo. (Before that it was on WriteFreely, and before that it was a shared Joplin note.)

If you want to know more about how I set this website up, or why I made it, don't hesitate to email me!
