I'm back to updating the site! Made things look a little bit more cheerful with some color, but it's definitely a work in progress. I'd like to add more fun things, maybe spice up the Spotify widget, and add some more "me" stuff (resume, stuff I've made etc.). The big news though is the [bookbug](/bookbug) page! I had so much fun reading The Master and Margarita (it may be one of my favorite books of all time 🤭) and I'm excited for Strangers on a Train.

Next up for me is finishing up Dubliners, picking my way through TMAM again, and, of course, starting Strangers on a Train.
