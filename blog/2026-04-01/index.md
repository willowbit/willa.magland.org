Hello!

It's, um, been a little bit since I've written here. I'm a little embarrassed about it—there are many times when I came close to posting but didn't quite get there. I think I'd like to go back to posting small updates like these. Of note since my last post, I've been [published](https://delawaretoday.com/life-style/spring-equinox/), I secured an internship, and I tried my new flower press for the first time. Expect pictures soon.

## Reading

The [London Review of Books](https://www.lrb.co.uk/) has a particularly tempting deal right now: $12 for 12 issues. I couldn't resist, and I got my first issue just a few days ago. It had an article on Willa Cather, which I was pleased to see, and read. I've been a reader of the LRB for a little while, ever since a member of my book club, Richard, generously began handing his off to me.

I like the weight of them, and the hyper-specific letters near the beginning.

The book club's current book is *City of Thieves* by David Benioff, and it's quite good so far.

## Thinking

About who I used to be, and who I am becoming. Wondering if my world religions professor feels anything remotely close to what he speaks about, and if I'm any different. Admiring what a beautiful new carpet I have on my now-modest floor, and how nice it is to sit on. Watching myself play the game of conversation, while pretending to be the smudge of a tree in some Japanese painting.
