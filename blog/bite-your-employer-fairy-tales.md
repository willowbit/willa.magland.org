Oh boy, I have a *lot* of links for this week, so saddle up. [Lots of good stuff on the inter-webs](https://youtu.be/klP9FtCe9oA). I didn't make a soup this week, but been on the lookout for the next recipe to try. By the way, if you're in need of soup content, check out [the Brothmonger](https://brothmailer.brothmonger.com/).

1. [**I'm sorry I bit you during my job interview**](https://www.mcsweeneys.net/articles/im-sorry-i-bit-you-during-my-job-interview). If you only click one link on this post, it should be this one. That's a lie, keep reading!

2. Check out the [**Wildlife Photographers of the Year winners**](https://www.npr.org/2023/10/11/1204913096/2023-wildlife-photographer-year-winning-images). Seriously, some stunning stuff. I can't decide if my favorite is the fireflies, or the hippos!

3. In the spirit of the spookier of the seasons, [**enjoy a fairy tale**](https://jeandavidmoisan.com/posts/by-the-light-of-the-moon/). Short on time? Here's a [shorter one](https://lovebloodrhetoric.com/2023/10/08/the-princess-and-the-toad/).

4. Okay, now I mean it, this is my favorite share this week: "[The Missouri French Creole Folk Tale of Chasse Galerite](https://vimeo.com/871252019)," animated beautifully by Brian Hawkings. It's a wonderful story, I highly recommend watching it.

5. I've heard that long ago, Spotify had a song-recommendation feature. [**Sent You A Song**](https://www.sentyouasong.com/submit) is a cute little site that allows you to preserve the intimacy of sharing music with another. Send someone you love a song!

6. I've never read the **NYT's Metropolitan Diary** before, but after reading [this wonderful piece](https://archive.ph/8OpaH), perhaps I will. Each story is so magical!

7. **Your weekly freebie alert:** information! And a ton of it. Ever since I had to rely on the scans of super niche magazines from the nineties for a research project, I've kept an eye out for that kind of stuff. I present to you, the newly digitalized, completely free [Whole Earth Index](https://wholeearth.info/). Look at all that stuff!

8. **We're all getting sick of the AI craze**, or have been for a while. [This pretty much sums it up](https://botharetrue.substack.com/p/every-app-that-adds-ai-looks-like).
